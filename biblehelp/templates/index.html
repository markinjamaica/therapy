How can we overcome harmful emotions?

Since our feelings primarily stem from our thoughts, we need to learn to control our thinking.

We are bringing every thought into captivity to make it obedient to the Christ.
- 2 Corinthians 10:5

Identify, Correct, Dismiss, Get Busy

  1. Identify the harmful thoughts. Example: "I am worthless
  2. Try to correct the harmful thought by reasoning on it.Example: "I am not worthless! God sent his Son to die for me!
  3. Dismiss the toxic thought. Reject it as the lie that it is.
  4. Get busy. Get immersed in something positive.
